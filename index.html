Yuzai / CaveKitz

Minecraft Server

CaveKitz was a server made by me, Witherred & Intallact, that gave you a random kit whenever you died. You were limited to a 200x200 area to mine, kill, and more. The original was shut down due to suspension by the ImpendMC RandomKits owner. It rebranded to Yuzai, but was shut down shortly after due to plugin issues. Averaged 10–35 players.
